About

Once not so long ago, Zack was eating ice cream in the bathroom. He thought if meat should be vegan free why should ice cream be any different? In 2018, RØG was born in San Francisco, using only 100% real meat products.

We think if meat should be vegan free, then ice cream should be vegan free too. RØG use state of the art technology to invent a vegan free ice cream made by dead animals. Unlike traditional ice cream using a mixture of vegan and non-vegan products, our ice cream uses cream made by extracting proteins from animals. Why don't we just use milk? Well, where is the fun in that.
